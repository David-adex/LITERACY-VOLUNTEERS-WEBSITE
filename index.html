Make A Donation

We help children to develop their literacy and communication skills and reach their full potential.

We are a registered charity that enriches young people's lives by assisting with their literacy, communication and social skills. We work in partnership with schools by providing trained and supported Volunteers who make reading and communicating enjoyable to those at risk of disengaging from education. We also run workshops for parents/carers in order to develop their confidence and ability to help their children read at home.

Why the community needs us

To compete in the modern world we need a fully educated and trained workforce, early intervention is a cost effective method to achieve this and to prevent wasted potential. If one child disengages from their education there is a cost to them and society as a whole. Literate children who, in turn, become parents are able to assist their own children to read and communicate and thus prevent further "lost" generations.

Our impact on the community

Every week, during term time, our Volunteers help some 1000 children in the city of Nottingham and the county of Nottinghamshire overcome the difficulties that they have with reading and communication and obtain these imperative life skills.To further our Mission we are continuing to run a series of Workshops for parents/careers to give them the confidence and knowledge to help their children read at home.

For £5 you will be donating a book or a game for a child for use by the Volunteer with children.

For £50 you will be donating a compendium of books and games for use by the Volunteer with children.

For £350 you will be donating the Schools Annual subscription for a Literacy Volunteer and travel expenses.
